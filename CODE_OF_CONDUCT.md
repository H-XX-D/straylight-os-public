# Code Of Conduct

This project expects professional, technical collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, respectful, and specific.
- Assume public comments may be read by new contributors.
- Critique code, docs, claims, and evidence rather than people.
- Keep discussions focused on the repository and the technical decision at hand.
- Disclose uncertainty when making security, build, or hardware claims.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information, credentials, host details, or personal paths.
- Deliberately misleading release, security, or compatibility claims.
- Repeated off-topic disruption of issues, pull requests, or reviews.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates these
expectations. Contributors who repeatedly disregard them may be blocked from
participating in the project.
